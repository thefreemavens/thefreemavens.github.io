Moral Culpability

Definition

Moral culpability is the determination of who is ultimately at fault, or deserving of blame for the commission of actions which resulted in harm or loss for others.

Etymology

The word Culpable means "at Fault, or Deserving of blame", it is derived from the Latin noun Culpa which means "Fault, or Blame".
