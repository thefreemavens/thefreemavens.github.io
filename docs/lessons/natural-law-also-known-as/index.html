Natural Law Also Known As...

Natural Law has had many different names in many different cultures and traditions throughout the world, during different times in history.

Law of Cause and Effect:

  1. Effect invariably follows cause.
  2. For every action, there exists an equal and opposing reaction.

Law of Attraction:

  1. The energy you emit is the energy you attract.
  2. Energy flows where attention goes.
  3. As you think, feel, and act so you shall be.

Karmic Law or Moral Law:

  1. You reap what you sow.

The Golden Rule

  1. Do not do to others as you would prefer not to have done to you.
